My Neighborhood

My neighborhood is a pretty cool place that's not that cool, honestly. That said, we have some nice local places I occasionally go to. There's this lovely little place called Antonella's Cafe that has good sandwiches and fresh bread.
There's also the Local Library, which I'm rather fond of. The entrance has a strange modern architecture I'm not sure I like, but the back portion has these beautiful stained glass windows filled with many scenes and characters from famous stories, some real and some fictional.
